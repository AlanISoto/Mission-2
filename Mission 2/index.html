Our Story

We have been serving Franklin since 2004 with amazing tapas and an atmosphere to match. Our recipes are authentic and time-tested. The ingredients are always fresh and the result is a menu that bursts with flavour.

Mission

Our mission is for everyone to experience the joy of eating fabulous, fresh food with friends, family and loved ones over a shared meal. Tapas means 'lids' and is a traditional Mediterranean way of eating shared snack size dishes. Its a delightful way to enjoy food as you can mix and match depending on your appetite and taste buds. By its nature tapas encourages conversation which is so important in a time where technology rules. Put down the phone, talk to your companion and enjoy a fabulous eating experience!
